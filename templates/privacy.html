Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we collect the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service: displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling you to manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics sold to third parties, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting) to operate the service, but these providers do not have access to your expense data and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channels. We are committed to addressing any privacy concerns promptly.
